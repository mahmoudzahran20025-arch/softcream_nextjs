## ğŸ¯ Design Philosophy

### Pickup View

**Priorities:**

2. **Contact** - Reach out if needed
3. **Progress** - See delivery stages

**Design Choices:**
- Delivery progress timeline
- Customer address displayed
- Delivery-specific status messages
- Branch shown as reference (nearest)
